Who We Are

We are a boutique digital design agency that blends creativity with technology to help brands stand out and businesses run smarter. Our focus is simple: create digital experiences that not only capture attention but also drive lasting impact.

Over the years, we’ve had the privilege of collaborating with companies across media, technology, and consumer industries, designing, building, and managing products that elevate their presence and streamline their workflows.

Our work is rooted in collaboration, curiosity, and craft. Whether it’s launching a new product, reimagining a user experience, or designing tools that make teams more efficient, we thrive on helping our partners bring bold ideas to life.

We’re always excited to collaborate with brands and organizations that are ready to create, grow, and innovate.

Aaron Silverstein
Founder & Principal Consultant

Aaron is a product strategist with a background in UX design, research, and software development, who has led digital projects for global brands and startups alike. He started L’abricot Labs with a team of designers and engineers to help mission-driven businesses turn bold ideas into impactful products.
